iOS 14.4 --- CSS BUG

Please use iOS 14.4 safari to view

container: blue-border-box
box1: red-background-box
box2: green-background-box

According to the given css, the height of box1 should be shrinked and box2 should be fully displayed.
But on iOS 14.4 safari the display is wrong, the height of box1 is not shrinked and box2 is displayed out of the container.

Conjecture

The test found that this happens when container and box1 both have `display: flex; flex-direction: column;`, and box1 have `justify-content: space-between or space-around or space-evenly;`.

height: 400px;

flex-shrink: 1;

display: flex;

flex-direction: column;

align-items: center;

justify-content: space-between;

height: 200px;

flex-shrink: 0;
